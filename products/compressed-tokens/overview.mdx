Compressed tokens are SPL-compatible tokens that store their state in Solana's cheaper ledger space instead of expensive account space. They work exactly like regular SPL tokens but cost significantly less to create and manage.

## Key Benefits

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Massive Cost Savings" icon="dollar-sign">
Create millions of token accounts for the price of hundreds with regular SPL tokens.
</Card>

<Card title="Full SPL Compatibility" icon="handshake">
  Compressed tokens follow the SPL token standard and work with existing Solana
  programs.
</Card>

<Card title="Wallet Support" icon="wallet">
  Supported natively by major wallets including Phantom, Backpack, and others.
</Card>

<Card title="Atomic Conversion" icon="arrows-rotate">
Seamlessly compress and decompress between regular and compressed tokens in a single transaction.
</Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  **Perfect for Airdrops**: Distribute tokens to thousands of users at minimal
  cost - ideal for rewards programs and marketing campaigns.
</Tip>

## Common Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Airdrops & Rewards">
Distribute tokens to large audiences cost-effectively. Perfect for marketing campaigns, community rewards, and user acquisition programs where you need to reach thousands of users without breaking the budget.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Gaming Tokens">
  Create in-game currencies and collectibles without breaking the bank. Ideal
  for web3 games that need to mint thousands of tokens for player rewards,
  achievements, and virtual economies.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Social Tokens">
  Issue community tokens and reputation systems affordably. Build tokenized
  communities where members can earn and trade social tokens based on
  participation and contributions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Loyalty Programs">
Build point systems and membership tokens efficiently. Create scalable loyalty programs for businesses that need to issue tokens to large customer bases.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
