# Privacy Policy

## Our Commitment to Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when using ZK Compression.

## Data Collection

### Minimal Data Collection

We only collect data that is necessary for the operation of our services. We follow the principle of data minimization to ensure we collect only what is needed.

### Types of Data

- **Technical Data**: Network identifiers, transaction hashes, and compressed account states
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymized usage patterns to improve our services
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect or store personal identifying information

## Data Handling

### User Control

Users maintain full control over their compressed accounts and associated data. You can:

- Access your compressed account data at any time
- Transfer or close compressed accounts as needed
- Control what data is shared with third-party applications

### Transparent Practices

All data handling practices are transparent and documented. We provide clear information about:

- What data we collect and why
- How data is processed and stored
- Who has access to different types of data

### Data Retention

- **Blockchain Data**: Permanent storage on Solana L1 for state commitments
- **Off-chain Data**: Stored only as long as necessary for service operation
- **Analytics Data**: Anonymized and aggregated data retained for service improvement

## Privacy Protections

### Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Our zero-knowledge proof system ensures that sensitive information remains private while still allowing for verification and validation.

### Encryption

All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols to prevent unauthorized access during transit.

### Access Controls

Strict access controls ensure that only authorized systems and personnel can access data, and only for legitimate operational purposes.

## Compliance

### Regulatory Compliance

- **GDPR Ready**: Our systems are designed to be compliant with GDPR requirements
- **Data Protection**: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures
- **Regular Reviews**: Privacy practices are reviewed and updated regularly

### User Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you may have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of data
- Withdraw consent where applicable
