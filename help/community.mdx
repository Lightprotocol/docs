#### CompressedSwap

- **GitHub**: [github.com/example/compressed-swap](https://github.com/example/compressed-swap)

## Community Guidelines

To maintain a welcoming and productive environment:

### Code of Conduct

- **Be respectful**: Treat everyone with kindness and respect
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome developers of all backgrounds and skill levels
- **Be helpful**: Share knowledge and assist others
- **Be constructive**: Provide thoughtful feedback and suggestions

### Communication Guidelines

- **Stay on topic**: Keep discussions relevant to the channel
- **Search first**: Check if your question has been answered before
- **Be clear**: Provide context and details when asking for help
- **Share resources**: Link to helpful documentation and examples

### Contribution Guidelines

- **Follow standards**: Adhere to coding standards and best practices
- **Test thoroughly**: Ensure your contributions are well-tested
- **Document changes**: Provide clear documentation for new features
- **Be patient**: Allow time for review and feedback
